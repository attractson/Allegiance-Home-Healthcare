About Us

Standing Drums has been a traditional performance in South Korea since the beginning of the century and it embodies the fluid blend between traditional Korean percussion and contemporary rhythm. The sequence is composed of single and double beats that allows the audience to feel the dominant energy. The pure yet empowering beat of the Standing Drum surrounds the atmosphere once the performance begins. The powerful beats play with distinct dynamics and driven tempo to create a performance that amazes the audiences’ ears. The harmonious and united movements engage the audiences’ attention. Together, Standing Drums captures the eyes and ears of the crowd.
